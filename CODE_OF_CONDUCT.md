# Code of Conduct

This project follows a Code of Conduct to foster a collaborative and respectful environment for contributors and users. All participants are expected to adhere to the standards below when interacting in project spaces (issues, pull requests, mailing lists, and forums).

## Expected behavior
- Be professional, courteous, and constructive.
- Use inclusive language and avoid assumptions about others.
- Respect other contributors' time and effort.
- Provide actionable, evidence-based feedback when reporting issues or reviewing changes.

## Unacceptable behavior
- Harassment, discriminatory language, or personal attacks.
- Threats of violence or any illegal activity.
- Posting private information or doxxing.
- Repeated disruption of discussions or harassment of other participants.

## Enforcement
Project maintainers are responsible for enforcing this policy. Instances of unacceptable behavior may result in warnings, removal of offending content, or banning from the project, depending on severity.

## Reporting
To report violations or request assistance, contact the project maintainers at: arn-c0de@protonmail.com. Include relevant context (links, screenshots, and steps) to help with triage.

This Code of Conduct is intended to support a constructive and inclusive community for working on ESP32-LABs.
